Our Approach

Three things most websites get wrong.

After years of building websites for Nigerian businesses, we've learned that almost every site fails in the same three ways. Here's what we do differently.

01

Designed for selling, not showing off

Every section has a job. Every CTA has a destination. We don't add features that look cool — we add ones that move people closer to a purchase.

02

Mobile-first, because Nigeria is mobile-first

Over 80% of your traffic will be on phones. We design for thumbs and slow data connections first — then scale up beautifully to desktop.

03

Fast — like genuinely fast

A 3-second load time costs you 40% of mobile visitors. Our sites typically load in under 1.5 seconds, even on 3G. Speed is conversion.
