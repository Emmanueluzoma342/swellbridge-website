01 · Landing Pages

Pages built to convert — not just to exist.

A landing page is a sales conversation in HTML. We write the copy, design the layout, choose the proof, and structure the CTAs based on direct-response principles tested by Eugene Schwartz, Sabri Suby, Russell Brunson, and Frank Kern — adapted for the Nigerian buyer.

  • Hero section that earns the next scroll
  • Pre-framing your offer so price feels small
  • Proof stack: testimonials, results, case studies
  • Objection-handling copy built into the page
  • Frictionless CTA: WhatsApp, form, or instant booking
  • Mobile-first design — because that's where Nigerians live

02 · Email Sequences

The follow-up most businesses never do.

In Nigeria, buyers need to trust you before they pay you. That trust is built between the moment they land on your page and the moment they pay — and 80% of it happens through email. We write nurture sequences that warm cold leads, address fears, and lead them to a confident decision.

  • 5–10 email welcome sequence for new leads
  • Storytelling emails (Frank Kern method) — not "blast" emails
  • Objection-handling sequences for cold prospects
  • Re-engagement campaigns to revive dead lists
  • Behavioural triggers (opens, clicks, page visits)
  • Setup on Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Brevo, or your preferred platform

03 · Lead Routing & Tracking

Know exactly where every naira is going.

A funnel without analytics is a wish. We wire up the tracking — Meta pixel, Google Analytics, conversion events, UTMs — so you can see precisely how every lead arrived, what they cost, and where in the funnel they dropped off.

  • Pixel + GA4 setup with proper event tracking
  • UTM parameter strategy for every campaign
  • Lead routing logic (qualified vs unqualified)
  • WhatsApp integration with pre-filled messages
  • Dashboards in Google Sheets or Looker Studio
  • Weekly performance reports — no fluff, just numbers
